Welcome to the
Choate Zoom Coordinator

This website allows students and teachers to easily save and access Zoom meeting IDs. Meeting IDs are displayed on buttons and link directly to the meeting.

Developers' note: Teacher names are not in the correct order due to inconsistencies in the way ITS gave the name of each teacher. Some of the names were given in reverse-order, others were given in an incorrect reverse order, and others were given in a different ordering scheme. Please be patient while we work to fix this.

Information for Teachers:

Teachers can set their office hours and classes Zoom meeting IDs. Any changes made by a teacher will overwrite a student's individual data.

Information for Students:

Students can set their classes Zoom meeting IDs, but these changes do not effect other students' data. In addition, students can view and search for a teacher's office hours ID when it is set.

Classes are ordered chronologically and display its starting time.
